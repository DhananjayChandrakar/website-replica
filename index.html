What is Ecosystem Restoration?

Ecosystem restoration means assisting in the recovery of ecosystems that have been degraded or destroyed, as well as conserving the ecosystems that are still intact. It can take many forms — from planting trees and greening cities, to rewilding gardens, changing diets, or cleaning up rivers and coasts.

Why It Matters

Every year we lose ecosystem services worth more than 10% of our global economic output due to land degradation. Restoring ecosystems can help fight climate change, conserve biodiversity, and enhance people’s livelihoods.
